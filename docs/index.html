SEC Self-Regulatory Organization Rulemaking Feed

An RSS feed of SEC SRO (Self-Regulatory Organization) rulemaking filings, automatically updated every 6 hours.

RSS Feed Atom Feed JSON

Sources

This feed aggregates filings from:

  • National Securities Exchanges (NYSE, NASDAQ, CBOE, etc.)
  • FINRA
Filters Applied:

The following types of filings are excluded:

  • "Notice of Filing and Immediate Effectiveness" - Routine filings that take effect immediately
  • Crypto/Digital Asset listings - Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrency-related filings

Usage

Add this feed URL to your RSS reader:
